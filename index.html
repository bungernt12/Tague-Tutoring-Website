About

Hi, I'm Tague. I love to teach.

I am passionate about tutoring and helping students grow. With a knack for breaking down complex concepts into understandable pieces, I guide students towards those 'aha' moments where everything clicks.

I believe in not just teaching the material but also in helping students develop effective study habits and life skills that will serve them well beyond their educational journey.

I also believe that in-person tutoring is more productive and personal than virtual. I have the tech to do Zoom sessions, and in some circumstances, that's the best option. However, when possible, I recommend face-to-face tutoring.
